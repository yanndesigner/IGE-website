ABOUT US

We are IDEAL GREEN ENGINEERING Ltd, (IGE) Ltd was established in 2012. IGE Ltd is a private limited liability company which was founded for the objective of offering a full technical and professional service package combining effective, efficient, and qualitative services regarding the study, survey, execution and Supervision of civil engineering projects, and hiring of construction machinery and commercialization in Rwanda. The founder has spent over 8 years working on exciting projects in Rwanda and it is through his extensive experience that the company was born.
